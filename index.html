Game Objective

The Objective of the game is to defeat the boss on floor 5 of the dungeon with the least amount of deaths. Each death will be recorded. When you are defeated, the people of the land will give you extra power to help you with your journey. Try talking to them before you're ready to try again.

How to fight

You are given 4 abilities to defeat your enemies. Check out your character sheet to see their descriptions! Once you have entered a battle, click on the enemy you want to attack and choose the ability to hit them with. Some abilities don't do any damage, these are status affect moves. They give you buffs to your character for a limited amount of turns, or for the entire battle. Each class has an ultimate ability that can be used once per battle. This ability will always be the fourth on your bar

Your Map

When you open your map you'll be given 3 places you can go to, two of them are towns with characters who can help you get stronger, the third option is the dungeon. Click on the dungeon when you're ready to give it your all.

Dialog Help

These characters are here to help you along your journey. Each time you are defeated one of these characters will give you a buff for your next run. To access these characters, open your map and go to a town. You will be given a list of all the characters and ask them for help. If they say they can't help you then go back to them again when you have died a few more times.
